My Education and Work Experience

I graduated from the University of Missouri in December 2014 with bachelor degrees in journalism and business. My journalism degree has an emphasis in multiplatform design, or designing across print and digital mediums, while my business degree has an emphasis in marketing. After graduating, I became a newspaper design at the Lee Enterprises Regional Design Center in Madison, Wisconsin. I held this position for two years before deciding to go back to school and focus on web design and development.

I started my course work at Anne Arundel Community College in January 2017. Since last year, I have taken classes introducing me to HTML and CSS, responsive web design, PHP and mySQL, Java, JavaScript, and jQuery. All of these courses have taken a hands-on approach so I have created 10+ multi-page websites for my web design classes and small applications for my programming ones. I will graduate in May 2018 with a Computer Science Associate's degree in Internet Applications Development.

In addition to school, I worked as a freelance web designer for a local business during the summer of 2017 and have been employed a start-up web design and software company since May 2017. In this position, I have planned and built websites for the company's clients, helped create the company's website, managed online and offline marketing efforts, and been involved in establishing company processes and procedures.

My Professional Goals

My professional goals are, honestly, a bit vague. After graduating, I would like to find a full-time position where I have the opportunity to expand my programming skills. I'm not opposed to having a position that involves creating content, such as writing blog posts, but I would like to do less of it. I enjoy designing websites. However, I'm also interested in improving the accessibility and user experience of websites and applications. My goal is to get more experience with front-end and back-end programming languages so that I can work in different areas of web and application development.
