About Us

"Explore: Get Out & Go" was started in 2000. Given the amount amount of military personnel and their families in the areas surrounding Baltimore, Annapolis, and Washington, D.C., we thought it would be nice if they had an up-to-date source for what there is to do in the area. There's no reason to sit at home when where's there so much to do nearby!

Our home page will be updated weekly with upcoming and seasonal events. Our other pages will be more constant and only updated a few times a year. We will focus on highlighting lesser-known activities or events since it is easy to find information for the major things.

If you know of an activity or event that we haven't mentioned, please let us know through our contact form. We are always happy to add more to our site.

— Jane, Paul, and Susan
