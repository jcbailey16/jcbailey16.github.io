Upcoming Events

Time for the Cherry Blossom Festival!

Spring has arrived, and so have the Cherry Blossoms! Head into to the nation's capital to see this amazing sight in person.

The 2017 National Cherry Blossom Festival will last from March 17 to April 15. The best time to see the blooms is expected to be between March 20 and March 24.

Downtown D.C., will host a variety of activities from fundraiser marathons, parades, and concerts during the festival month. There will be a high-volume of visitors, as people come from around the world to the see the cherry blossoms. Consider taking the D.C. metro.

Saturday farmers market returns

Starting in April, the Anne Arundel County Farmers Market will be around for the whole weekend.

  • Saturdays:7am to Noon
  • Sundays:10am to 1pm

The farmers market is located at 275 Harry S. Truman Parkway, Annapolis, MD 21401.

Baltimore Scavenger Hunt

Join us June 24 to June 30 for a city-wide scavenger hunt!

How to participate:
  1. Sign up
  2. Download the Urban Quest Adventure app to your phone
  3. Finish challenges and earn points
  4. Have fun!

We are putting together a team to participate in Urban Adventure Quest's annual scavenger hunt. We can have between 20 and 30 people on our team. Spots go quickly for this free event, so sign up soon!

This event is free and will continue rain or shine. More information will be sent via email once you sign up for the team.

Last year we came the closest yet to completing the scavenger hunt, finishing with only five items left unfound. Our goal for this year is to find all the items by the end of the final day. We can't wait to beat our record and make more fun memories!

For the sign-up form: this activity is the "Baltimore Scavenger Hunt 2017."
