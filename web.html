Raving Software

Raving Software (View Site )

Raving Software is a web design company, I worked at from 2017 - 2018. Part of my responsibilities were planning, designing, and coding websites for our clients and for the company's website. All the other websites listed in the "Raving Software" section are websites I worked on for clients.

Marie Chantal's Bakery (View Site )

For this small bakery located in Mt. Airy, Maryland, we developed a consistent theme through establishing a color scheme, font choices, and repeated elements. In addition, we improved her online ordering and reviewing processes, and trained the client on how to update her photo galleries and the slider that runs with the reviews using the Wordpress CMS.

The Delightful Cupcake (View Site )

This is a website I built on my own as a theme example for Raving Software. We wanted to showcase our abilities and give clients an idea of what we could do for their website. Having examples of other websites helps the client to figure out what they do and don't like and what might work best for their business needs.

Silver Star Service Center, Inc. (View Site )

This was the first website I helped to create while employed at Raving Software. We built the website to assist in the business's workflow by incorporating service appointment scheduling, a vehicle sales section, a customer rating capaiblity and customer contact forms, all of which can be accessed by the client through the Wordpress CMS.
