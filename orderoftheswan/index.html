News & Announcements

Ticket sales will go to help the SPCA

Published March 25, 2017

A portion of all ticket sales from our summer tour will be donated to the SPCA. We are very excited to partner with this organization as animal welfare is an important issue to us. Check out your local SPCA website for more information on what they do and how you can get involved. We hope to see you all at one of our stops! Tickets are on sale for all tour locations, but pay attention to our Facebook and Instagram accounts for special offers.


Published March 15, 2017

Summer tour dates announced

After a long winter break, we are happy to announce our summer tour, "Harmony of Silence." We'll kickoff of the tour in late May, starting in Chicago. We're still finalizing a few things, so more shows will be added to the schedule over the next few weeks.We are excited to get back on the road and perform live again. And don't worry, we did some work over the break and have a few new songs for you guys. You'll love them and we'll have studio versions available for digital download in late summer 2017. Follow us on Facebook and Twitter for music and tour news.

Published March 5, 2017

St. Patrick's Day sale, contest

From March 5 to March 18, all Order of the Swan merchandise will be 25% off. That includes CDs, clothes, posters, and other accessories! Plus, take a picture of yourself with your favorite Order of the Swan merchandise and post it to either our Facebook or Instagram account for a chance to win two free tickets and backstage passes to any future show. Make sure to tag us (@OOTSofficial) and use the hashtag "#FreeTicket" so we see your entry. The contest ends at midnight on March 31. Winners will be announced the week of April 10.
