Lee Enterprises

Time of Employment: January 2015 — December 2016

Position: Page Designer

Description of Position: As a newspaper page designer at the Madison Regional Design Center, I used Adobe InDesign and Total Blox CMS to design pages for 30+ newspaper publications. I often produced 15-30 pages per day while working on multiple deadlines and also reviewing colleagues’ pages for copy editing and design errors.
