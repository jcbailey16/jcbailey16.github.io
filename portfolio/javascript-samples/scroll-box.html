Christmas Presents

I know, it's Nov. 6, why am I buying Christmas presents already?

First, I like to be done by Thanksgiving. The earlier you shop, the more likely you are to get the things people want. Plus, shopping in November is a nightmare no matter, so it's best to get things done early. If you really need to wait for the sales, make sure you can order the items online. Fighting Black Friday crowds isn't fun for anyone.

Second, we are having an early Christmas with my brother and sister-in-law when we go to visit them in Mississippi for Thanksgiving. We'll still have regular Christmas at home, but I need to have presents for when we drive down in a few weeks.

Third, fall semester finals are just before Christmas. If I can get one stress factor out of the way early, then I focus all my attention on finals and final projects at the beginning of December.
