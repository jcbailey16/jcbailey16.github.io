Print Design

On this page, you will find samples of my print design work from a magazine design course I took at the University of Missouri.

Due to the nature of the newspaper deadlines and the large volume of pages produced on a nightly basis, I often did not have the time to save work samples before they were deleted from the system. In addition, Lee Enterprises uses templates to speed up the design so I've not included the few samples I have because I don't feel they represent my design abilities. While I was a newspaper designer, though, I regularly designed news, feature, sports, and special sections, including covers.
